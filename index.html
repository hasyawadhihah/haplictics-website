About World Happiness Index

What is the World Happiness Index?

The World Happiness Index measures happiness across countries. It is based on the World Happiness Report published by the United Nations.

The report started in 2012, but this study uses data from 2015 to 2025. It helps compare global happiness and well-being over time.

The Six Factors

Factors that influence the
World Happiness Report

Each country is scored across six dimensions that together paint a complete picture of national well-being.

Upload factor1.jpg

GDP Per Capita

Average income per person in a country, showing economic strength and the living standards citizens can realistically achieve.

Upload factor2.jpg

Social Support

The perceived ability to count on friends or family during difficult times.

Upload factor3.jpg

Healthy Life Expectancy

How long people can expect to live in good physical and mental health.

Upload factor4.jpg

Generosity

Willingness to help others, such as through donations or kind actions.

Upload factor5.jpg

Freedom

The ability to make personal life choices and live based on one's own decisions.

Upload factor6.jpg

Corruption Perception

How much people believe their government and institutions are honest and trustworthy.
